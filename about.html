Get To Know Me

Culture Curator | Entrepreneur | Traveler

Mai Sari

I am a designer and illustrator who considers my work to be a form of activism. Society is in need of a cultural shift that inspires engagement beyond instant gratification and that mentality shows through all of my work.

I am passionate about projects deeply rooted in our collective growth, community, awareness, and those striving to cultivate a positive long-term impact. For the past 8 years I have been freelancing, which mostly consisted of branding and illustration. A majority of my clients have been involved in the maker movement, STEAM education, co-working spaces, code schools, non-profits, and people working on really awesome, local centric projects. Recently my main focus has been working on UI/UX and I continue to develop my skills in this area as well as my illustrations.
